# Sample IVR — Call Flow Description

When a customer calls, they hear a welcome greeting and are presented with three options: press 1 (or say "Sales") to reach the sales team, press 2 (or say "Support") to reach the support team, or press 3 (or say "Voicemail") to leave a message directly. If the caller doesn't make a valid selection, the system will re-prompt them once before automatically sending them to voicemail.

When routed to Sales or Support, the system plays a brief hold message and rings the team member's phone for up to 20 seconds. If no one picks up, the caller is automatically forwarded to voicemail where they can leave a message up to 60 seconds long. Every call is recorded from the moment it's answered for quality assurance and training purposes.

All voicemail recordings and call recordings are stored in your SignalWire dashboard and can be reviewed at any time.
